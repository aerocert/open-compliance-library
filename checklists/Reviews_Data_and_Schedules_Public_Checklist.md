# Reviews Data and Schedules Public Checklist

## Section 1 — Your Formal Reviews

_Based on DO-178B/C §7 and §8 review discipline, in your milestone reviews:_
- **(1)** Have you planned the key reviews — Requirements Review, Preliminary Design Review, Critical Design Review, and Test Readiness Review?
- **(2)** At each review, do you confirm the right data is ready, and capture issues and track them to closure?
## Section 2 — Project Schedules and Resources

_Based on DO-178B/C §11.2 planning, does your project plan:_
- **(1)** Show the major software milestones and how the life-cycle phases transition?
- **(2)** Plan certification liaison — when and what you will submit to the FAA?
- **(3)** Identify the people, tools, and facilities (the resources) needed to do the work?
## Section 3 — General Software Data and Delivery

_Across all your life-cycle data:_
- **(1)** Is the data complete, controlled, and clearly identified, so a reviewer can find what they need?
- **(2)** Is the data marked with the right status and delivered in the agreed form?
## Section 4 — Previously Developed and COTS Software

_Based on DO-178B/C §12.1, if you reuse software:_
- **(1)** Have you shown reused or COTS software meets the objectives for your software level — not just that it worked before?
- **(2)** Have you assessed the impact of any modification to it, or of using it in a changed environment?
- **(3)** If you rely on product service history, do you have the data to support that argument (relevant environment, enough hours, problem records)?
  - Note: This free checklist is a high-level sanity check — free to use and share. It doesn't include the item-level actions needed to fully close each objective; those live in AeroCert's comprehensive checklist, kept current as the standards change and available from AeroCert.
