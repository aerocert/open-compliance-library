# Software Plans Public Checklist

## Section 1 — Your Development Plan (SDP)

_Based on DO-178B/C §11.2, does your Software Development Plan:_
- **(1)** Describe how the software is organized and built — the life cycle, the processes, and who is responsible?
- **(2)** Define the transition criteria between phases (how you know one activity is complete enough to start the next)?
- **(3)** Identify the development environment — the programming languages, compilers, and tools you will use?
- **(4)** Reference the standards the project will follow?
## Section 2 — Your Verification Plan (SVP)

_Based on DO-178B/C §11.3, does your Software Verification Plan:_
- **(1)** Describe how you will verify the software — the reviews, analyses, and tests — and who performs them independently where your software level requires it?
- **(2)** Define the verification environment and the criteria for when verification is complete?
## Section 3 — Your Configuration Management Plan (SCMP)

_Based on DO-178B/C §11.4, does your Software Configuration Management Plan:_
- **(1)** Describe what is placed under configuration control, and at what points (the baselines)?
- **(2)** Describe problem reporting, change control, and change review?
- **(3)** Cover archive, retrieval, release, and how the software is loaded and protected?
## Section 4 — Your Quality Assurance Plan (SQAP)

_Based on DO-178B/C §11.5, does your Software Quality Assurance Plan:_
- **(1)** Describe how QA will gain assurance that the processes and their outputs follow your plans and standards?
- **(2)** Provide for QA independence and for a conformity review before you declare compliance?
## Section 5 — Your Standards

_Based on DO-178B/C §11.6 through §11.8, do you have:_
- **(1)** Requirements, design, and coding standards that set the rules and constraints your team will follow?
- **(2)** Coding standards appropriate to the actual programming language(s) you are using?
## Section 6 — Making Sure the Plans Hold Together

_A cross-check across all your plans:_
- **(1)** Are the plans consistent with each other and with the PSAC — same processes, same life cycle, same software levels?
- **(2)** Does the rigor in the plans match the software level (more verification and more independence at the higher levels)?
  - Note: This free checklist is a high-level sanity check — free to use and share. It doesn't include the item-level actions needed to fully close each objective; those live in AeroCert's comprehensive checklist, kept current as the standards change and available through donations.
